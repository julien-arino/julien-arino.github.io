Annour Saad Abdramane is a PhD student in Mathematics at the Université de N'Djaména in Chad. He is co-supervised by Julien Arino, Mahamat Salah Daoussa Haggar (Université de N'Djaména, Chad) and Patrick Memphis Tchepmo Djomegni (Northwest University, Potchefstroom, South Africa).

Annour Saad works on models of spread of cholera, focusing on the situation in the Lake Chad Basin.

- A metapopulation model for the spread of cholera. AS Abramane, J Arino, PM Tchepmo Djomegni & MS Daoussa Haggar. Submitted and [arXiv](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2505.17269), 2025.
- A model for cholera with infectiousness of deceased individuals and vaccination. AS Abramane, J Arino, PM Tchepmo Djomegni & MS Daoussa Haggar. Submitted and [arXiv](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2504.07432), 2025.
- Dose-limited interventions in an epidemiological model. AS Abramane, H Djimramadji, PM Tchepmo Djomegni, MS Daoussa Haggar & J Arino. Submitted and [arXiv](), 2026.
